李 念 慈

Taiwan & Vancouver

Emmy Cheng works at the intersection of oil painting and paper clay — two mediums that share an intimacy with texture, weight, and the slow unfolding of form.

Her work is drawn deeply from the natural world, especially plants and the quiet life they carry. Based between Taiwan and Vancouver, she moves between two landscapes — each with its own light, its own green.
